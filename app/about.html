Paintopia founder Tiffany Chang

The Paintopia event lets you try painting in a fun and relaxing way, and make new friends.

We host regular member events in Shanghai and Tokyo. Everyone is welcome to join.

Paintopia is also a great team building event. We can tailor make a half or full day with art competitions and games, or just a regular painting with a glass of wine.

If your company wishes to sponsor a Paintopia event we can offer various sponsor packages. Restaurant and hotel sponsors can simply offer a space for one of our events.

    Many of our events are for charity. One of our fundamental reasons for being is so that we can give back to the community that we work in.

    How we got started

    Paintopia was started in 2012 by Tiffany Chang, who has organized similar art events for the past few years in Shanghai. Outside of Paintopia, Tiffany works in the luxury industry in China.
