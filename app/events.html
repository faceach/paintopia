PAINTOPIA EVENTS

We hold monthly events, in Shanghai and in Tokyo. The events run for about 3-4 hours, and you can come and go as you please. No stress. Paint at your own speed and however you like.
It’s not a competition, and you don’t have to share what you are painting with others.
After the event you bring your painting home, with you.
